About

RACKLANTA Health is an initiative to make Racklanta more health concious.

Racklanta Health makes you healthy, happy and makes you MOVE.

Racklanta Health plans on conducting lots of fun events, sports tournaments, healthy meal days etc. If you have a personal health goal to acheive, Racklanta Health will help you with that as well.

Some of the activities planned are writing a healthy cookbook, slowly replacing breakfast with healthier options, bringing in healthier drinks in lieu of sodas, conducting fun challenges, and allowing Rackers to acheive personal health goals.

Racklanta Health will be inaugurated on Friday, August 23 2013 during our Annual Picnic. During the annual picnic, Racklanta Health table will have healthier food alternatives. Enjoy!

Ping Pong Tournament

  • Racklanta 2013 Ping Pong Tournament will happen between Sep 21 and Oct 30.
  • Brackets will be drawn via lottery.
  • There will be a wildcard round for early eliminators.

Rules:

  • Each match will be a best of 3 games, 21 points each.
  • If a player doesnt want to play, walk over, opponent automatically wins.
  • Umpriring will be provided on demand.
  • Try to schedule games at a mutually amicable time for both players and not interfering your regular work.
  • You can bring your own paddles.

Walkers Club

Physical activity doesn't need to be complicated. Something as simple as a daily brisk walk can help you live a healthier life.

For example, regular brisk walking can help you:

  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Prevent or manage various conditions, including heart disease, high blood pressure and type 2 diabetes
  • Strengthen your bones
  • Lift your mood
  • Improve your balance and coordination
  • The faster, farther and more frequently you walk, the greater the benefits.
